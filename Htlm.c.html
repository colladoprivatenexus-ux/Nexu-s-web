About Us

Your Global Partner in Business Excellence

Based in the vibrant business hub of Miami, Florida, Collado Nexus has evolved into a premier consulting firm with a distinctive international presence. Our team of expert agents extends across Brazil, Mexico, Dominican Republic, and Puerto Rico, creating a robust network that understands both local markets and global dynamics.

We specialize in managing the complex intersection of financial strategy, marketing innovation, advertising excellence, and logistics optimization. Our unique approach to import and export solutions, combined with our expertise in artist and brand management, sets us apart in the consulting landscape.

Whether you're a startup seeking strategic direction or an established enterprise looking to expand globally, Collado Nexus provides the expertise, insights, and connections necessary to unlock your business potential.
